About

I'm a Senior Data Scientist and a trainer in Industrial Statistics with a focus on:

  • Design of Experiments
  • Measurement System Analysis
  • Manufacturing Process Control

My experience in the manufacturing industry spans 22 years, first with Philip Morris, then Rolex and now Nestlé. Having held numerous positions in R&D, Operations and IT I have gathered a broad understanding of how consumer goods are conceived, industrialized and manufactured.

I have a background in Mechanical Engineering from the Instituto Superior Tecnico of Lisbon and a specialization in Applied Data Science by the Swiss technical university EPFL. This helps me getting deep insights in many different industrial contexts.
